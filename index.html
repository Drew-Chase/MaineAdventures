About US

Howdy Campers!

We thought we would take a moment to introduce ourselves to those who do not know us yet. We are into our 6th year as owners of Allagash Gateway Campground & Cabins.

We are the Scanlins, Steve, BeLinda, Kayte, Keenan & Maddi Mae. We left the "city" behind, as an independent contractor & a nurse, to grow a new life in the great outdoors.

We hope to provide an experience that will give families lasting memories of camping, hunting, hiking, waterway, snow activities, and so much more of enjoying all that Maine has to offer.

If you have an idea of what you might like to do, please let us know and we will do our best to prepare an experience to your expectations.
